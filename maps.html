Maps

Artistic map of the city of Toronto

I have used OpenStreetMap data and Inkscape to create this map of the city of Toronto, focusing on the downtown core and the Toronto islands.

Transit lines and desired riders in Waterloo Region

Using QGIS, OpenStreetMap, Canadian census and GRT shapefile data, I have mapped neighbourhoods in Waterlo Region with higher incomes and share of population over 25 against the frequent bus network operated by GRT.

Houston current and projected LRT network

For my thesis at the University of British Columbia, I have mapped the existing and proposed light rail metro lines in Houston, Texas. Using METRORail shapefile data and OpenStreetMap, this map was used to analyze changes in housing prices and income along newly built rail corridors.

Income changes in transit-adjacent neighbourhoods in Houston

Using US census data, I have analyzed changes in census tract level incomes in Houston across the time frame during which additional transit lines were constructed.
